Wind Speed


Based on the results of the data set and the graph above, there does not appear to be a strong correlation between latitude and wind speed, indicating that latitude does not affect wind speed. The graph above illustrates that areas both far and near to the equator can experience either low and high wind speeds. What is interesting, though, is that very high wind speeds (above 25 mph) only occur in the northern hemisphere at latitudes greater than 45 Deg.
